## Free Cash Flow (Non-GAAP Reconciliation)

Free cash flow is a non-GAAP financial measure calculated as net cash from operating activities
less purchases of property, plant, and equipment.

|                                                          | 2023    | 2022    | 2021    |
|----------------------------------------------------------|---------|---------|---------|
| Net cash provided by operating activities (GAAP)         | $1,892  | $1,623  | $1,398  |
| Less: Purchases of PP&E                                  | ($312)  | ($287)  | ($261)  |
| **Free cash flow (non-GAAP)**                            | $1,580  | $1,336  | $1,137  |
| FCF margin (FCF / Revenue)                               | 24.4%   | 23.9%   | 22.5%   |
| FCF conversion (FCF / Net income)                        | 114.9%  | 114.8%  | 112.8%  |

Free cash flow represents our ability to generate cash after investments required to maintain and
grow the business. Free cash flow is used to fund share repurchases, dividends, debt repayments,
and strategic acquisitions.
