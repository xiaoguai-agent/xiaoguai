## Notes to Financial Statements (Selected)

### Revenue Recognition (ASC 606)

The Company recognizes revenue when control of promised goods or services is transferred to
customers in an amount that reflects the consideration the Company expects to be entitled to in
exchange for those goods or services.

**Product Revenue**: Recognized at a point in time upon delivery and acceptance by the customer.
Variable consideration (rebates, volume discounts) estimated using the expected value method and
constrained to the amount unlikely to result in a significant revenue reversal.

**Service Revenue**: Professional services recognized over time using an input method (labor
hours incurred relative to total estimated hours). Subscription and maintenance recognized
ratably over the contract term.

**Remaining Performance Obligations**: As of December 31, 2023, remaining performance obligations
were approximately $2.1 billion, of which approximately 60% is expected to be recognized as
revenue over the next 12 months.

### Earnings Per Share

Basic EPS is computed by dividing net income attributable to common stockholders by the weighted-
average number of common shares outstanding during the period. Diluted EPS includes the dilutive
effect of stock options and RSUs calculated using the treasury stock method.

Anti-dilutive securities excluded from diluted EPS calculation: 2.1 million shares for 2023.
