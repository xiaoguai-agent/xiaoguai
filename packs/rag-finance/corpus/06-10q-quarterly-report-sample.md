## Liquidity Update

## Risk Factors Update

No material changes to risk factors since our Annual Report on Form 10-K for the year ended
December 31, 2022, except as noted below:

**Macroeconomic uncertainty**: Continued uncertainty in global macroeconomic conditions,
including the impacts of rising interest rates, foreign currency fluctuations, and potential
recessionary pressures in certain markets, may affect demand for our products and services.
In Q3 2023, we saw modestly longer sales cycles in EMEA for transactions exceeding $500,000.

**AI-related competitive dynamics**: The rapid emergence of generative AI capabilities has
increased competitive intensity in several of our product categories. We are investing
significantly in AI features to maintain and extend our competitive position; however, there is
no assurance that these investments will be successful.
