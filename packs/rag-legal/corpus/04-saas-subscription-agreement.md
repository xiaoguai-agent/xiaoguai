SOFTWARE AS A SERVICE SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This Software as a Service Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between
[VENDOR NAME] ("Vendor") and [CUSTOMER NAME] ("Customer") as of [DATE] ("Effective Date").

## 1. Services

### 1.1 Subscription
Subject to Customer's timely payment of fees and compliance with this Agreement, Vendor grants
Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, limited right to access and use the software service
identified in the applicable Order Form (the "Service") during the Subscription Term solely for
Customer's internal business purposes.

### 1.2 Restrictions
Customer shall not, directly or indirectly:
(a) sublicense, resell, or distribute the Service or any component thereof;
(b) modify, translate, adapt, or create derivative works based on the Service;
(c) reverse engineer, disassemble, decompile, or otherwise attempt to derive the source code;
(d) use the Service to build a competing product or service;
(e) remove or alter any proprietary notices on the Service.

## 2. Service Level Agreement

### 2.1 Uptime Commitment
Vendor will use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Service available 99.9% of the time
in any given calendar month ("Monthly Uptime Percentage"), excluding Scheduled Downtime.

### 2.2 Scheduled Downtime
Vendor will provide at least 48 hours' advance notice of any Scheduled Downtime. Scheduled
Downtime will not exceed 8 hours per calendar month.

### 2.3 Service Credits
If Monthly Uptime Percentage falls below 99.9%, Customer is eligible for service credits:

| Monthly Uptime % | Credit (% of Monthly Fee) |
|---|---|
| 99.0% – 99.9% | 5% |
| 95.0% – 99.0% | 10% |
| Below 95.0% | 25% |

Service credits are Customer's sole and exclusive remedy for any failure to meet the uptime
commitment.

## 3. Fees and Payment

### 3.1 Fees
Customer agrees to pay Vendor the fees set forth in the applicable Order Form ("Fees"). All
Fees are non-refundable except as expressly set forth in this Agreement.

### 3.2 Payment Terms
Fees are due and payable within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. Past-due amounts accrue
interest at 1.5% per month (or the highest rate permitted by law, whichever is lower).

### 3.3 Taxes
Customer is responsible for all taxes, duties, and similar assessments arising out of this
Agreement, excluding taxes based on Vendor's net income.

## 4. Data and Privacy

### 4.1 Customer Data
Customer retains all rights, title, and interest in and to its data submitted to the Service
("Customer Data"). Vendor will process Customer Data only to provide the Service and as directed
by Customer.

### 4.2 Data Security
Vendor will maintain commercially reasonable administrative, physical, and technical safeguards
for the protection, confidentiality, and integrity of Customer Data, consistent with industry
standards including SOC 2 Type II controls.

### 4.3 Data Return and Deletion
Upon termination or expiration, Vendor will make Customer Data available for export for 30 days,
after which Vendor may delete Customer Data. Vendor will certify such deletion upon request.

## 5. Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Vendor IP
Vendor retains all rights, title, and interest in the Service, including all software, algorithms,
interfaces, and documentation. This Agreement does not grant Customer any ownership rights.

### 5.2 Feedback
If Customer provides suggestions, feedback, or recommendations regarding the Service ("Feedback"),
Vendor may use Feedback without restriction and Customer hereby assigns all rights in Feedback to
Vendor.

### 5.3 Aggregated Data
Vendor may collect and use aggregated and anonymized data derived from Customer's use of the
Service for product improvement, benchmarking, and industry analysis, provided such data cannot
reasonably identify Customer or any individual.

## 6. Confidentiality

Each Party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other Party's Confidential Information
using at least the same degree of care it uses for its own similar information, but no less than
reasonable care. Neither Party will disclose Confidential Information to any third party without
prior written consent, except to employees or contractors who need to know for purposes of this
Agreement and are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

## 7. Term and Termination

### 7.1 Term
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for the initial Subscription Term
specified in the Order Form, and renews automatically for successive one-year terms unless either
Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days before the end of the then-current
term.

### 7.2 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if:
(a) the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within 30
days after written notice describing the breach;
(b) the other Party becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy protection.

### 7.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination, Customer's right to access the Service immediately ceases. Sections 3, 5, 6,
8, and 9 survive termination.

## 8. Limitation of Liability

IN NO EVENT WILL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, EXEMPLARY, OR
CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES, REGARDLESS OF THE FORM OF ACTION AND WHETHER OR NOT SUCH DAMAGES WERE
FORESEEABLE. VENDOR'S TOTAL LIABILITY ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT
EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY CUSTOMER IN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

## 9. Warranty Disclaimer

THE SERVICE IS PROVIDED "AS IS." VENDOR EXPRESSLY DISCLAIMS ALL WARRANTIES, WHETHER EXPRESS,
IMPLIED, STATUTORY, OR OTHERWISE, INCLUDING ANY IMPLIED WARRANTY OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR
A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, TITLE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT.

## 10. General

### 10.1 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to conflict of
law principles.

### 10.2 Entire Agreement
This Agreement, including any Order Forms, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties
and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous representations, warranties, or agreements.

### 10.3 Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions
continue in full force and effect.
