## Offer of Employment

We are pleased to offer you the position of [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY NAME] ("Company"), reporting
to [MANAGER NAME], [MANAGER TITLE]. We believe your skills and experience will be a valuable
addition to our team.

## Start Date

Your anticipated start date is [START DATE], contingent upon satisfactory completion of a
background check and your execution of the documents described below.

## Compensation

**Base Salary:** Your annual base salary will be $[AMOUNT], payable in accordance with the
Company's standard payroll schedule (bi-weekly). This salary will be subject to all applicable
federal, state, and local tax withholdings.

**Bonus:** You will be eligible to participate in the Company's discretionary annual bonus program,
with a target bonus of [X]% of your annual base salary. Bonus eligibility requires active employment
on the date of payment. The Company retains sole discretion to modify, suspend, or discontinue
any bonus program at any time.

**Equity:** Subject to approval by the Board of Directors, you will be granted an option to
purchase [NUMBER] shares of the Company's common stock at an exercise price equal to the fair
market value per share as determined by the Board on the grant date. Options vest over a four-year
period, with a one-year cliff: 25% of your options will vest on the first anniversary of your
start date and the remaining 75% will vest monthly over the following 36 months, subject to
continuous service. All equity grants are subject to the terms of the Company's equity incentive
plan and your option agreement.

## Benefits

You will be eligible to participate in the Company's standard benefit programs, including health,
dental, and vision insurance, 401(k) plan, and [X] days of paid time off per year, subject to the
terms and conditions of those plans, which the Company may modify at any time.

## At-Will Employment

Your employment with the Company is at-will, meaning either you or the Company may terminate
the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause, and with or without advance notice.
Nothing in this letter or any other Company document creates a contract of employment for any
specified period.

## Conditions of Employment

This offer is conditioned upon:

1. **Background Check:** Satisfactory completion of a background investigation, the results of
which are acceptable to the Company in its sole discretion.

2. **Work Authorization:** Your submission of documentation establishing your eligibility to work
in the United States as required by the Immigration Reform and Control Act of 1986.

3. **Proprietary Information and Invention Assignment Agreement (PIIA):** Your execution of the
Company's standard PIIA prior to your start date. The PIIA covers, among other things:
   - Your obligation to keep the Company's confidential information confidential during and after
     your employment;
   - Your assignment to the Company of all inventions, works of authorship, and other intellectual
     property created during your employment that relate to the Company's current or anticipated
     business.

4. **Non-Compete / Non-Solicit:** You will be asked to sign the Company's standard agreement
prohibiting solicitation of Company employees and customers for a period of 12 months following
termination of employment. Enforceability varies by jurisdiction; please review with counsel.

## Expiration

This offer will expire if not accepted by [EXPIRATION DATE]. Please indicate your acceptance by
signing and returning a copy of this letter by that date.

We are excited about the prospect of you joining our team and look forward to building something
great together.

Sincerely,
