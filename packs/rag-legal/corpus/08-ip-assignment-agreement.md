INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE]
between [COMPANY NAME], a [STATE] corporation ("Company"), and [ASSIGNOR NAME] ("Assignor").

## 1. Definitions

"Intellectual Property" means all inventions, discoveries, designs, developments, methods,
modifications, improvements, processes, know-how, trade secrets, works of authorship, software
(including source code, object code, and documentation), databases, trademarks, and any other
intellectual property or proprietary rights.

"Company IP" means all Intellectual Property that Assignor:
(a) creates, discovers, conceives, develops, or reduces to practice, alone or with others,
during Assignor's engagement with Company;
(b) creates using Company resources, equipment, facilities, or Confidential Information; or
(c) relates to Company's actual or reasonably anticipated business, research, or development
at the time of creation.

## 2. Assignment

Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in and to all
Company IP, including all patent rights, copyrights, trade secret rights, and all other
intellectual property rights therein, throughout the world.

## 3. Works Made for Hire

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, all Company IP that constitutes copyrightable
subject matter is a "work made for hire" as defined in 17 U.S.C. § 101, and Company is the
author and owner of all copyright in such works. To the extent any Company IP does not qualify
as a work made for hire, Assignor hereby assigns all copyright in such works to Company pursuant
to Section 2.

## 4. Moral Rights Waiver

To the extent permitted by applicable law, Assignor waives and agrees never to assert any moral
rights in Company IP, including rights of attribution and integrity.

## 5. Prior Inventions

Assignor represents and warrants that attached as Exhibit A is a complete list of all Intellectual
Property that Assignor has, alone or jointly with others, conceived, developed, or reduced to
practice prior to engagement with Company, and that Assignor wishes to exclude from the scope of
this Agreement ("Prior Inventions"). If no Prior Inventions are listed, Assignor represents that
there are none. Company does not acquire any rights to Prior Inventions.

## 6. Assistance

Assignor agrees to assist Company in every proper way to evidence, record, and perfect the
assignments and to apply for, obtain, maintain, enforce, and defend any rights assigned. Assignor
appoints Company as its attorney-in-fact to execute documents if Assignor fails or is unable to
do so after reasonable request.

## 7. Representations and Warranties

Assignor represents and warrants that:
(a) Assignor has the authority to enter into and perform this Agreement;
(b) Assignor has no obligations to any third party that conflict with this Agreement;
(c) Company IP does not infringe any third-party intellectual property right, to Assignor's
knowledge;
(d) Company IP is free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and other restrictions on assignment.

## 8. Survival

This Agreement survives termination of Assignor's engagement with Company with respect to all
Company IP created prior to such termination.

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE], without regard to conflict of law principles.
